Welcome.

 

This website will take you on a journey through 19th century Scotland. It will visit graveyards and amphitheaters, murderers and doctors.

This is a period of history where the relationship between England and Scotland was changing, when fears of the consequences of scientific advancement were at a high, and when cities were stigmatized as places of sin and violence.

Through this medium, we hope to give a wider view of the many facets of life which influenced this infamous case.

Explore webpages and follow the breadcrumb trail that will allow you to string together what happened in Edinburgh, 1828. This game is open-ended and based upon concepts of exploration and discovery. There is no set ending.
